Our Story

Over Two Decades of Creating Magical Wedding Experiences

Founded in 2003, WeddingBliss has been at the forefront of transforming wedding celebrations in Mumbai. What started as a single venue has now blossomed into multiple venues that cater to various preferences, themes, and budgets.

Our journey has been guided by our passion for making every wedding an unforgettable experience. We understand the significance of this special day and strive to provide venues that reflect the dreams and aspirations of every couple.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional venues and services that transform wedding celebrations into timeless memories. We aim to understand the unique vision of every couple and bring it to life through our carefully designed spaces and attention to detail.

Our Vision

To be the premier destination for wedding celebrations in Mumbai, known for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and personalized service. We aspire to continuously evolve our venues to reflect the changing trends while preserving the essence of traditional celebrations.
